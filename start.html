What Seedwork Does

1

A conversation, not a template

A 5-minute AI-guided session that helps you get the idea out of your head and into a plan you can actually use. It asks you the hard questions — "Who specifically would pay for this?" "What can you do this week?" — and doesn't let you off the hook.

2

A plan you'll actually follow

The plan is just the beginning. Seedwork checks in with you: "You said you'd reach out to 10 customers this week. How did it go?" It helps you adjust when reality doesn't match the plan — because it never does.

3

A support group that keeps you honest

You get matched with a small group of people working on their own ideas — not a Facebook group with thousands of strangers. You check in regularly, share what's working (and what's not), and keep each other moving. Think of it like a study group, but for building something real.
